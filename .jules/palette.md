## 2025-02-12 - Context-Aware Empty States
**Learning:** Generic "no items found" text is a missed opportunity for user guidance, especially when filtering by different modes (Code vs Cowork) or by specific accounts. Providing context-aware icons and explaining *why* the state is empty (e.g. "Abra o Claude Desktop e inicie uma conversa no Cowork...") significantly improves user understanding of system state.
**Action:** Always include mode-specific or context-aware messaging in empty states rather than generic fallbacks. Include a call-to-action or explanation of how to populate the list.
